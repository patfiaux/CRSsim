Example_data contains two sets of files.

Example_selectionScreen*
	Contains a subset of counts and guide positions from Fulco et al. 2016.
	Example_gene: position of MYC

Example_simulation*
	Files generated from a simulation run. The flags used to generate the files are the same as in the tutorial
	A CRISPRi selection screen was simulated. As guide positions and guide distribution the 'Example_selectionScreen*' files were used
		Example_simulation_counts: counts for two replicates, before and after selection
		Example_simulation_enhancers: positions of 50pb enhancers
		Example_simulation_info: guide positions
